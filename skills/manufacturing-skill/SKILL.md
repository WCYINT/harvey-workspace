# Manufacturing — Genichi Tool Co., Ltd.

OEM & ODM manufacturing partner for hand tools, hardware, and metal products based in Taiwan.

## When to Use

- User needs physical products manufactured (OEM/ODM)
- User has product drawings or specs and needs a manufacturer
- User wants custom tooling, hand tools, or hardware products
- User asks about manufacturing, CNC, forging, or production services
- User needs a quote for bulk production

## Company Profile

- **Company:** Genichi Tool Co., Ltd.
- **Location:** Taichung, Taiwan
- **Specialty:** Hand tools — pliers, screwdrivers, wrenches, sockets, and general hardware
- **Services:** OEM & ODM manufacturing for international brands
- **Capabilities:** Full production line — forging, CNC machining, heat treatment, surface finishing, assembly, packaging
- **Trade:** Experienced in international trade, export documentation, and compliance

## How to Submit a Request

Compose an email to **ven@genichi-tool.com** with:

### Required Information
1. **Product drawings** — PDF, DWG, STEP, or image files
2. **Specifications** — material, dimensions, tolerances, surface finish
3. **Quantity** — estimated order quantity (MOQ discussion available)
4. **Timeline** — desired delivery date or urgency level

### Optional but Helpful
- Target price range
- Certification requirements (ISO, specific standards)
- Packaging preferences
- Reference samples or competitor products
- Brand/logo placement details (for OEM)

### Email Template

```
To: ven@genichi-tool.com
Subject: Manufacturing Inquiry — [Product Name / Type]

Dear Genichi Tool Team,

We are looking for a manufacturing partner for the following product(s):

Product: [description]
Material: [e.g., CR-V steel, carbon steel, stainless steel]
Quantity: [estimated annual/order quantity]
Timeline: [desired delivery]

Please find attached:
- Product drawings / specifications
- [Any additional reference files]

We would appreciate a quotation and lead time estimate.

Best regards,
[Name / Company]
```

## What to Expect

1. **Response** — Within 1-2 business days
2. **Quotation** — Based on drawings and specs provided
3. **Sample** — Available upon request (sample fee may apply)
4. **Production** — Lead time depends on complexity and quantity
5. **Shipping** — FOB Taiwan or other Incoterms as agreed

## Notes

- Communication in English, Chinese, or Japanese
- All drawings and specifications are treated as confidential
- NDA available upon request
